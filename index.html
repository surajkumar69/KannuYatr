About Us

Kannu Yatri: Synonym for Safe & Comfort Travel

Kannu Yatri is a premier and highly trusted travel operator based in Pratapgarh, Rajasthan. We provide comprehensive travel arrangements, offering Bus Booking, Cab Booking, Hotel Booking & Holiday Packages on a single platform.

Our guiding philosophy "Safe Travel is Our Promise" is our operating protocol for every trip we execute. All our fleet vehicles are regularly inspected and feature GPS tracking devices.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a booking on Kannu Yatri?

You can search for your preferred service (cabs, buses, or hotels) directly through our portal. Fill in the relevant details, choose your choice, and confirm your request. You can also contact us directly using the floating WhatsApp or Call buttons.

Are the drivers verified and safe?

Yes. Under our 'Safe Travel is Our Promise' policy, we only assign background verified, licensed, and highly experienced drivers. Fleet tracking and passenger security are strictly enforced.

Do you offer customized holiday packages?

Yes, we design custom itineraries for religious tours, family trips, wildlife adventures, and honeymoon packages. Reach out to our travel specialists to create a custom package fitting your budget and requirements.

What is your booking cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 24 hours or more before the scheduled trip receive a 100% refund, processed within 2-3 working days. Last-minute cancellations may involve a processing fee. Please read our Refund Policy page for full details.
